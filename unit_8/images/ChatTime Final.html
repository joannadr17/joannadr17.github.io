Empowering Communication

Mobile Capabilities Offering A Voice

  • Common Phrase Selection
  • Text-to-Speech
  • Eye Tracking

For patients with neurodegenerative diseases such as ALS, the ability to speak and communicate declines as the disease progresses. Introducing ChatTime, a user-friendly and intuitive interface, which provides AAC capabilities for individuals with complex communication needs. This mobile application leverages text-to speech capabilities through speech recognition and synthesis for mobile devices. By combining a visual, descriptive interface with these capabilities, patients are empowered to communicate as effectively as possible despite limitations imposed on them by the disease.
